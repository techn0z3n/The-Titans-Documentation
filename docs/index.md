This documentation is dedicated to the **"Almost there!"** update (v0.495) for Minecraft 1.7.10. Use the navigation above or the quick links below to explore the mod's mechanics, bosses, and dimensions.

!!! info "Project Scope"
    This wiki focuses specifically on the technical stats and survival strategies for version 0.495, known for the "You Reap What You Sow" mechanics and the introduction of the Nowhere.
!!! danger "Warning"
    This mod contains entities capable of crashing your game or banning you from your own world. Always maintain backups!
