### 0.495: Almost there!
* **Boss UI:** Added custom boss bars for all Titans!!
* **Compatibility:** Added cross-mod content support.
* **Agriculture:** Added the first custom crops: **Malgrum** and **Pleasant Blade**.
* **Ender Colossus:** Now equipped with **EYE LASORS!!**
* **Mechanics:** Re-designed the Titan regeneration system and buffed overall stats.
* **The Ultima Blade:** Re-modeled the blade; it now performs a **worthiness check** before use.
* **Equipment:** Added a health boost effect to all Titan-tier armors.

### 0.49: Hooray! Legit content!
* **Animations:** Fully animated the **Ultima Iron Golem Titan**.
* **Combat:** Added a stun method for the Ender Colossus and updated its crystals.
* **Anti-Cheat:** Titans can now **kick players** for cheating or attempting to bypass mechanics.
* **Balance:** Slime and Magma Cube Titans no longer have damage limits.
* **Optimization:** Reduced registered entities; **OreSpawn** compatibility is restored.
* **Minions:** Added **Bishops**, a new powerful minion type.
* **Buffs:** Increased health for Giant, Wither, and Ender Dragon minions.
