* **Body Slam:** Massive impact dealing 500 damage.

!!! warning "Enraged Phase"
    Once the Omegafish drops below **1/4 health**, it will begin causing **random explosions** at the player's feet. Stay mobile to avoid instant death.

### The Burrowing Mechanic
The Omegafish can burrow into the ground to close the distance instantly. 
* **Invulnerability:** While burrowed, this Titan is immune to **all damage types**.
* **Bug Note:** In some early pre-builds of 0.49, a bug could cause the Omegafish to remain permanently invulnerable if it was hit while transitioning into a burrow.

## Minions:
The Omegafish is never alone. It will constantly summon its minions:
* **Silverfish Loyalists:** Basic fodder. Attacks everything their particular Titan would attack, even if the Titan isn't visibly there.
* **Silverfish Priests:** Support units. Heal nearby injured allies.
* **Silverfish Zealots:** Aggressive attackers. Capable of performing super-human feats such as sprinting really fast, jumping higher than normal, taking more injures than it's possible to sustain, and killing entities many times bigger than themselves.
* **Silverfish Bishop:** They bring down judgement from the heavens onto the enemies of their titan.
* **Silverfish Templar:** Tanky defenders. The most complicated and powerful of the five types. They are capable of levitation, are immune to fire and poison, and have extremely high resistance to magic damage. They also alternate between their normal mode of attack, and special random ranged attacks. Their is a list for all of these random attacks for each Templar linked [here](../minions/templar_attacks.md)

## üéí Strategy & Loot

### How to Defeat
1. **The Stun:** You cannot melee it safely at first. Use [Harcadium Arrows](../items/harcadium_arrow.md) to chip away at it.
2. **The Flop:** Once it sustains enough damage, it will "flop" over, becoming stunned.
3. **The Finishing Move:** This is your chance to use your titan-slaying tools for maximum damage. Keep in mind it won't stay stunned forever

### Taming the Beast
Unlike Witherzilla, the Omegafish can be subdued. 
1. Stun the Omegafish.
2. Right-click with a **Golden Apple**.
3. If successful, you can mount and control this Titan.

### Notable Drops
* **All things Silverfish Related:** Stone, Silverfish Blocks, and Books.
* **Harcadium Nuggets:** Rare chance to drop materials for mid-tier gear.
