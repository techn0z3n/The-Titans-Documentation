# The Omegafish

* Health: 8000 (4000 hearts)
* Status: [*Lesser Titan*](ranks/lesser_titans.md)

## Attacks 
* Head Butt (50 damage)
* Tail Swipe (50-200 damage)
* Lightning Shot (50+burning)
* Tail Smash (400)
* Body Slam (500)
* Random explosions at your feet if below 1/4 health
* Can burrow to get to a target much faster, while burrowed, immune to all damage types
### Fun fact 
* There used to be a bug where the Omegafish became permanently invulnerable due to the burrowing mechanic

## Minions 
* Silverfish Loyalists
* Silverfish Priests
* Silverfish Zealots
* Silverfish Templar

## Healing Factor
* Lower

## Drops
* A whole lot of random stuff, including things associated with Silverfish (stone, books, etc.)
* Way of hurting it: shoot him with [Harcadium Arrows](items/harcadium_arrow.md) until he flops over, then attack him

## Description
The weakest of the Titans, the Omegafish is still no push over in a fight. Wearing diamond armor alone won't cut it against even this monstrosity. This one can be mounted and controlled after stunning it and right it with a golden apple to subdue it.
